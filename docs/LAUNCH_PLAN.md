# Permabullish Launch Plan

**Version:** 1.0
**Created:** January 31, 2026

---

## Launch Philosophy

> The launch will not be an "event" — it will be a **prolonged enterprise** stretching over several weeks.

The product itself becomes the artifact shared for launch. Every piece of content demonstrates real value.

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Types

#### 1. Demo Videos (High Production)
- Screen recordings showing product interaction
- Clean, professional editing
- Clear voiceover/captions
- 2-5 minutes each

#### 2. Use Case Videos
| Theme | Example Title | Hook |
|-------|---------------|------|
| Discovery | "I asked AI to analyze this small-cap stock" | Curiosity |
| Comparison | "AI vs Broker Research: Who got it right?" | Controversy |
| Education | "How to read an AI stock report" | Value |
| Results | "This AI predicted 40% upside - here's what happened" | Proof |
| Speed | "Full research report in 30 seconds" | Convenience |

#### 3. Quick Tips / Shorts
- 15-60 second clips
- Single feature highlight
- Works for Reels, YouTube Shorts, Twitter

#### 4. User Stories (Later)
- Testimonials from early users
- Before/after portfolio decisions
- Community success stories
