Investment Thesis

We are aggressively bullish on Bharti Airtel because it's hitting a perfect trifecta: domestic tariff hikes finally sticking, African operations turning profitable, and 5G monetization beginning to show results. With promoter holding jumping 4.3 percentage points while FIIs lighten up, this screams insider confidence meeting temporary foreign jitters - a classic buying opportunity.
