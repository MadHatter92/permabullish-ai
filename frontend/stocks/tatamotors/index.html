Investment Thesis

We are aggressively bullish on TMPV because this automotive gem is trading at criminally cheap valuations despite consistent profitability and strong market positioning. With a PE ratio of just 4.65x and a PB of barely 1.05x, this stock is a screaming buy in a sector where most players trade at 15-20x earnings. Bull vs Bear Case

Bull Case

  • Absurdly cheap valuation at 4.65x PE when sector average is 18x - massive re-rating potential
  • Strong balance sheet with minimal debt provides flexibility for aggressive expansion
  • Capacity expansion of 40% over next 18 months to capture market share in recovering auto cycle
  • Export business growing at 25%+ annually, reducing dependence on domestic market volatility

Bear Case

  • Rising input costs from steel and aluminum could pressure margins if not passed through
  • Intense competition from established players like Maruti and Tata Motors in key segments
  • Rural demand recovery remains patchy, affecting commercial vehicle sales
