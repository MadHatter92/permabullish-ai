Investment Thesis

We are aggressively bullish on L&T because it sits at the epicenter of India's multi-decade infrastructure boom with an unmatched execution track record. With order book visibility extending 3+ years, margins expanding consistently, and FIIs doubling down with a 600 basis point stake increase, this is a generational wealth creator trading at reasonable valuations. The company's diversification into green energy, data centers, and defense positions it perfectly for the next growth wave.

Bull vs Bear Case

Bull Case

  • Order book of ₹4+ lakh crores provides 3+ years revenue visibility with 85% domestic exposure insulating from global headwinds
  • Operating margins expanded from 10.2% to 13.3% over the past year due to improved project mix and operational efficiency
  • FII ownership surged from 19.5% to 25.5% indicating strong institutional confidence and potential for further re-rating
  • Diversification into high-growth verticals like data centers, green hydrogen, and defense reduces cyclical construction dependency

Bear Case

  • Trading at 5.3x book value appears rich compared to historical averages of 3-4x during previous cycles
  • Working capital intensive business model can strain cash flows during rapid growth phases
  • Execution risks remain elevated given the scale of projects and potential for cost inflation in commodity prices
