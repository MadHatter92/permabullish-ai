Investment Thesis

We are aggressively bullish on NTPC because it's the ultimate infrastructure play on India's unstoppable energy demand story. With 25% revenue growth in recent quarters, aggressive renewable expansion adding 60+ GW by 2032, and a ridiculously cheap valuation at 19x PE for a monopolistic cash cow, this is generational wealth creation hiding in plain sight. The green transition isn't a threat - it's NTPC's next growth chapter.

Bull vs Bear Case

Bull Case

  • Dominant 25% market share in India's power generation with 75+ GW capacity creating unmatched economies of scale
  • Revenue growth accelerating to 25%+ driven by 78% plant load factors vs industry average of 65%
  • ₹2 lakh crore green capex plan adding 60 GW renewables by 2032, transforming from coal giant to energy conglomerate
  • Trading at discount 19x PE vs 22x sector average despite superior margins and government backing

Bear Case

  • Heavy coal dependence (70%+ capacity) faces long-term ESG and regulatory headwinds globally
  • State electricity board payment delays create working capital pressures, though government backing mitigates risk
  • Renewable transition requires massive capex potentially straining balance sheet despite strong cash flows
