While everyone's been obsessing over HDFC Bank's safe-but-slow approach, Axis Bank just quietly delivered its best quarterly performance in years with ROA hitting multi-year highs. Investment Thesis

We are aggressively bullish on Axis Bank because it's executing a flawless turnaround story that the market is still undervaluing. With credit costs normalizing, digital transformation accelerating, and ROA approaching 2%+ levels, this bank is firing on all cylinders. Trading at just 16x PE versus 18-20x for peers despite superior growth momentum is a gift that won't last long.

Bull vs Bear Case

Bull Case

  • ROA improvement trajectory continues with current levels at 1.8% and clear path to 2%+ within 12 months
  • Digital banking revenues growing at 40%+ annually, now contributing over 35% of fee income
  • Credit cost normalization to sub-40 bps provides ₹2,000+ crores annual earnings tailwind
  • Market share gains in corporate lending with 16% YoY growth versus sector average of 12%

Bear Case

  • Rising interest rates could pressure NIMs despite current strength at 4.1%
  • Intense competition in retail lending may compress spreads in key segments
  • Regulatory scrutiny on digital lending practices could impact fintech partnerships
