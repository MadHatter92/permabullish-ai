Investment Thesis

We are aggressively bullish on Bajaj Finance because this is India's most dominant consumer lending franchise firing on all cylinders. With consistent 25%+ profit growth, expanding margins, and an unmatched brand moat, this stock is criminally undervalued at 33x PE for a business growing at 26% annually. The PEG ratio of 0.017 screams 'BUY' louder than a town crier.

Bull vs Bear Case

Bull Case

  • Exceptional profit growth of 25.9% CAGR over 5 years with 30.9% median sales growth over 10 years
  • Massive profit margin of 45% - among the highest in global consumer finance
  • Strong promoter confidence with stake increasing to 54.8% from 54.7%
  • Healthy dividend payout of 17.4% while reinvesting aggressively for growth

Bear Case

  • Trading at 5.74x book value suggests rich valuation on asset-heavy metrics
  • Low interest coverage ratio could pose risks in rising rate environment
  • FII stake declined from 22.0% to 20.9%, indicating some institutional profit-taking
