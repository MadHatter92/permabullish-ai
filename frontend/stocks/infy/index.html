Picture this: While your neighborhood restaurant is still figuring out how to take orders online, Infosys is helping Fortune 500 companies build entire digital universes in the metaverse. The company that started as India's outsourcing poster child has quietly transformed into a full-stack digital transformation powerhouse, and the market is finally waking up to this reality.

Investment Thesis

We are aggressively bullish on Infosys because it's no longer just a cost arbitrage play - it's become the digital architect for global enterprises navigating AI and cloud transformation. With generative AI services gaining massive traction, robust deal wins in Q3 FY26, and expanding margins despite wage inflation, INFY is firing on all cylinders. The company is trading at just 23x earnings despite 15%+ growth visibility - a valuation disconnect that won't last.

Bull vs Bear Case

Bull Case

  • Generative AI services revenue run-rate of $150M+ with 300+ active engagements, targeting $1B+ by FY28
  • Operating margins at 21%+ despite wage inflation, demonstrating pricing power and automation benefits
  • Large deal TCV of $9.5B in first 9 months of FY26, up 25% YoY with average deal size increasing
  • North America revenue growth accelerating to 8.2% QoQ, signaling tech spending recovery in largest market

Bear Case

  • European revenue still contracting at 2.1% QoQ due to macro headwinds and reduced discretionary spending
  • Attrition at 12.1% remains elevated, creating wage pressure and project delivery risks
  • Increased competition from global players like Accenture and local players in AI services space
